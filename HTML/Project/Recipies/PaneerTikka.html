Paneer Tikka

About the dish

Paneer tikka is a popular vegetarian Indian appetizer made from paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in a blend of spices and yogurt, then grilled or baked to perfection. Here's some detailed information about it

Ingredients

  • Paneer: Cubed Indian cottage cheese.
  • Yogurt (Curd): Used for marination.
  • Spices: Common spices include turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, and chaat masala.
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: For flavor.
  • Lemon Juice: Adds a tangy flavor.
  • Vegetables: Often includes bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes, cut into chunks.
  • Oil or Butter: For grilling or baking.

    • Steps

    1. Marination: Paneer cubes are marinated in a mixture of yogurt, spices, ginger-garlic paste, and lemon juice. The marination time can range from 30 minutes to overnight, allowing the flavors to infuse.
    2. Skewering: Marinated paneer and vegetables are threaded onto skewers.
    3. Cooking: The skewers can be grilled, baked in an oven, or cooked on a stovetop grill pan until the paneer is slightly charred and cooked through.
